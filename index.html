Dicuss your favorite Albums

Once a review is published it will be accessible to users all across the platform. Users can take this opportunity to openly discuss their opinions about the albums they reviewed.

While we are all going to like different things, having a conversation and hearing conflicting opinions allows an individual to grow. We can learn alot about our music taste and discover life-changing albums by discussing music with everyone, even those we may disagree with!

Owl Bums is dedicated to making sure that all users always have a backlog of album reccomendations so that users never run out of new music to listen to and experience. Reccomended Albums will be based of a users reviews.
